# Sauti-Shamba — Voice Farm Advisor 🌾🎙️

**Sauti-Shamba** is a friendly, AI-powered voice advisor designed specifically for Kenyan smallholder farmers. It provides real-time, actionable advice on market prices, weather forecasts, and crop health in the languages farmers use most: **Swahili, Sheng, and English**.

---

## 🚀 Key Features

### 🎙️ AI Voice Advisor (Sauti)
Get expert farming advice just by speaking. Sauti-Shamba uses advanced AI to understand your questions and respond with clear, spoken guidance.

### 💬 Interactive Chat
Prefer typing? Use the chat interface to ask about planting schedules, pest control, or fertilizer recommendations.

### 🌦️ Localized Weather Forecasts
Stay ahead of the seasons with accurate weather updates tailored to your specific farming location.

### 📈 Real-Time Market Prices
Know the value of your produce before you get to the market. Access up-to-date prices for crops across various Kenyan regions.

### 🩺 Crop Health Diagnostics
Describe symptoms or ask about specific pests to receive immediate diagnostic advice and treatment options.

## 🛠️ Technology Stack

- **Frontend**: React 19 with TypeScript
- **Backend**: Express.js (Node.js)
- **Styling**: Tailwind CSS 4
- **Animations**: Motion (formerly Framer Motion)
- **Database/Auth**: Firebase (Firestore & Authentication)
- **AI Engine**: Google Gemini AI (via `@google/genai`)
- **Content Rendering**: React Markdown
- **Icons**: Lucide React

---

## 📋 How to Use

1.  **Login**: Sign in securely using your Google account.
2.  **Choose Your Mode**: Switch between **Voice (Sauti)** for hands-free advice or **Chat** for text-based interaction.
3.  **Ask Away**: Ask questions like:
    *   *"Bei ya mahindi ikoje leo Nairobi?"* (What is the price of maize today in Nairobi?)
    *   *"Mbona majani ya nyanya yangu yanageuka manjano?"* (Why are my tomato leaves turning yellow?)
    *   *"Kutanyesha wiki hii?"* (Will it rain this week?)
4.  **View History**: Access your previous conversations to review advice you've received in the past.

---

## 🔒 Security & Privacy

- **Secure Authentication**: Powered by Firebase Auth.
- **Data Protection**: Your conversation history is stored securely in Firestore with strict security rules, ensuring only you can access your data.

## 🌍 Impact

Sauti-Shamba aims to bridge the information gap for smallholder farmers, empowering them with the data they need to increase yields, reduce losses, and get better prices for their hard work.
